# Greenfield Ventures -- Strategic Plan 2026

## Mission

Enable data-driven decision making for mid-market B2B companies through accessible, actionable analytics.

## H1 2026 Priorities

### 1. Close Series A Funding
- **Target**: $8-12M at $20-22M pre-money valuation
- **Lead**: Ridge Capital (confirmed interest)
- **Timeline**: Term sheet by Feb 15, close by Mar 31
- **Use of funds**: 60% engineering hiring, 25% go-to-market, 15% operations

### 2. Reach 50 Enterprise Customers
- **Current**: 34 customers
- **Gap**: 16 new customers in 6 months
- **Strategy**: Focus on manufacturing and logistics verticals
- **Key metric**: Net revenue retention > 110%

### 3. Achieve SOC 2 Type II Certification
- **Prerequisite for**: Enterprise sales above $50K ARR
- **Timeline**: Gap assessment by Mar 1, audit by June 1, certification by Aug 1
- **Owner**: Marcus Webb (CTO)

## H2 2026 Priorities

### 4. Launch Self-Serve Tier
- Target SMB market with automated onboarding
- Price point: $199-499/month
- Reduce CAC for smaller deals from $340 to < $100

### 5. International Expansion (Evaluation)
- Assess EU market opportunity
- GDPR compliance review
- Potential UK office if metrics support it
