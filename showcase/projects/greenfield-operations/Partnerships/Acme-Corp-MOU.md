# Memorandum of Understanding
## Between Greenfield Ventures, Inc. and Acme Corporation

**Effective Date**: June 15, 2025
**Expiration**: June 14, 2026
**MOU Reference**: GV-ACME-2025-001

## 1. Purpose

This MOU establishes the framework for a strategic partnership between Greenfield Ventures ("GV") and Acme Corporation ("Acme") to jointly deliver analytics capabilities to enterprise customers in the manufacturing sector.

## 2. Scope of Partnership

### 2.1 Integration
Acme grants GV API access to its customer data pipeline for the purpose of providing analytics services to joint customers. Access is rate-limited to 10,000 requests per day.

### 2.2 Joint Go-to-Market
Both parties will co-market the integrated solution to manufacturing enterprises. Marketing materials require mutual approval.

### 2.3 Customer Support
Each party designates one integration engineer for ongoing support. GV designates Marcus Webb (CTO). Acme designates Jennifer Park (VP Engineering).

## 3. Financial Terms

### 3.1 Revenue Sharing
Revenue from joint customers is shared 85/15 (Acme/GV) based on the customer's primary contract holder.

### 3.2 Billing
Each party invoices its own customers directly. Revenue share is reconciled quarterly.

### 3.3 No Minimum Commitment
Neither party guarantees minimum revenue or customer referrals.

## 4. Data and Privacy

### 4.1 Data Sharing
Only aggregated, anonymized analytics data may be shared between parties. No personally identifiable information (PII) is exchanged.

### 4.2 Compliance
Both parties maintain their own compliance obligations (SOC 2, GDPR where applicable).

## 5. Intellectual Property

Each party retains ownership of its pre-existing IP. Joint developments are co-owned with mutual license grants.

## 6. Exclusivity

This agreement is non-exclusive. Either party may enter similar agreements with third parties.

## 7. Term and Termination

### 7.1 Term
This MOU is effective for one year from the Effective Date.

### 7.2 Renewal
Renewal requires 30 days written notice prior to expiration. In the absence of notice, the agreement continues month-to-month on existing terms.

### 7.3 Termination
Either party may terminate with 60 days written notice. Customer commitments made during the term will be honored.
